New Blogging Paradigm: Drafts

21 January 2022
1 minute read

One of the challenges for blogging consistently is finding the time to do it and make good posts. Posts like “Who is Satoshi” or “YC Bookmarklet” were simple enough to knock out, so I did.

For some time I’ve wanted to write about in depth topics like self-driving cars. This is what I’ll post next; and while I try to keep a positive vibe on this blog, that next post will be rather critical.

In order to accomplish this, instead of spending a lot of time crafting a blog post, I will post drafts and revise them. Each post will start at 0.1 and maybe one day it’ll reach 1.0.
